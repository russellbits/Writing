Field Note Sketch Prompts

Hazarabi

Using a style in medium of a textured field sketchbook page draw and image of a Hazarabi. The art must exhibit dense graphite and ink linework blended with soft, smoky colored pencil and charcoal washes in a low-contrast, earthy color palette. The composition should include anatomical studies, and quick, gestural annotations that look like rapid field observation, prioritizing a gritty, textural, and organically grotesque realism where the character looks like part of the landscape (sand, dust, wind, stones). The character’s face should be covered with goggles and scarves.


Synthezoids, Prompt 1
Based on these images for anatomy, not style, I would like you to create a field note sketch that has a 1m child next to a 1.85m average adult and a 2m large adult. The field note sketch should exhibit light graphite and ink linework blended with soft, smoky colored pencil and charcoal washes in a low-contrast, earthy color palette. The sketches are impressionistic in that they were drawn fast. The subjects of the drawings are known as synthezoids, a generation of robots that have now built themselves. They are functional in appearance, parts exposed and in some cases microscopic. Their most obvious feature is their eyes, which are wide circles of light. Everything else about them is "humanoid", bipedal, and they are capable of facial expressions.
