## 4. Harnesses

Common agent coding harnesses include [Claude Code](https://code.claude.com/docs/en/overview), [Codex](https://developers.openai.com), [Cursor](https://cursor.com), [Deep Agents](https://deepwiki.com/langchain-ai/deepagents), [OpenCode](https://opencode.ai), [Letta Code](https://docs.letta.com/letta-code), and [Pi](https://pi.dev). The list will likely continue. The term is often used more broadly to mean the non-model layer around a *language* model, so lists can also include tools like LangGraph or other agent runtimes depending on how narrowly you define “harness.” It is likely in the next few years that new models along the lines of JEPA will begin to change the reasoning levels of these models; however, the problem of convincing those models to use you methods, will still remain.
