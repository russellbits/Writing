Probably like you, I have more often been completely perplexed, as opposed to amused, regarding the daily comics pages in most newspapers. Marmaduke's multilayered meanings and sometimes crucial symbolism in particular often leave me pondering the meaning--unsure that I was smart enough to understand it. Luckily, Joe Mathelete has come to my rescue with his excelent blog, [Joe Mathlete explains Marmaduke](http://marmadukeexplained.blogspot.com/).
