When sad devotions
To lonely puddles
Evaporate like us
You keep dancing
And worlds wreck
Like mighty trains
Track to tight turn
All seems lost and
Still you dance
I sit like Ghandi
Starving, thoughtful
But a single glance
Shows me
Still you dance
