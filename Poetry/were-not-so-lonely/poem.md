title: We're Not So Lonely
alternate-title:
source: Day Dreams
date: 06-06-1997

Two lonely strangers  
met by and by  
as strangers often do  

but when they meet  
they both realize  
that lonely can't be two  

so close  
they do become  
though no soft words are spoken

and heart-felt thanks  
is felt between  
when no lonely hearts are broken
